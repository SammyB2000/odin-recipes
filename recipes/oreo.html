Milk and Oreos

Description

This is a delicious and simple snack I used to make as a child. I included it in a compilation of recipes my class made in kindergarten.

Ingredients

  • Oreos
  • Milk
  • Bowl

Steps

  1. Get Oreos
  2. Get milk
  3. Get bowl
  4. Pour milk into bowl
  5. Dip Oreos into milk and enjoy!
